my official tier list of fruits

by derek

today, we will be taking an analytical look at the fruit of the earth :D

theres lots of fruits, some big, some small. some are red, some are blue. some might be round, some might be spiky. but they're all fruit. so, what makes a fruit a fruit?

a fruit is when a flower gets pollinated by a bee and becomes the home for a seed. they're edible and tasty because they've evolved for animals to eat and spread the seeds!

now, we're going to rank some of the most popular fruit! i'm only going to rank six of them, with one for each respective tier ranking.

our fruits that we will be ranking today (in alphebetical order) are:

  1. apples

  2. lemons

  3. limes

  4. mangoes

  5. peaches

  6. watermelons
